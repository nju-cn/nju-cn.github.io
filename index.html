Biography

I am a Ph.D student in Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University advised by assitant Professor Sheng Zhang. My research interests are broadly in edge computing and machine learning. Recently, my work has focused on applying ML to edge computing to solve some fundamental problems. In particular, my research includes two directions --(1) AI for scheduling: using reinforcement to schedule the request at edges and (2) video analysis and streaming optimation: using AI to improve the object detection system or video streaming performance.
